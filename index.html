Internal Medicine Primary Care in Flint, Michigan: Adolescent, Adult, & Geriatric Health Care
Dr. Sahouri is a board certified Internal Medicine physician, or Internist, who has been in practice in Flint and Genesee County since 1993. As a primary care physician, Dr. Sahouri is experienced in the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of most medical conditions.

Adolescents as young as 12 years of age, and adults from young adulthood to advanced age are treated. Besides taking care of you and your family in the office, Dr. Sahouri will attend to your medical needs during your hospitalization with an acute illness, after surgery, and back in the office for post-hospital care. Our goal is taking care of you for life. That's Internal Medicine. Explore our Site, stop by the office, or call to schedule an appointment.
